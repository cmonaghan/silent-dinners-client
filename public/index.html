Join a Silent Dinner

Join a silent dinner in the tradition of the Plum Village Monastery. Hosts open up their homes to 6-10 guests for a homemade vegetarian meal; the first 15 minutes are enjoyed in silence, followed by mindful conversation and getting to know your dinner mates. Community-organized and 100% nonprofit.

How It Works

1 Sign up for a dinner

Find a local dinner near you. Sign up to notify the host and reserve your spot.

2 Get matched with a host

Once you are matched, you'll be asked to send $10 to your host via venmo or paypal to cover the cost of food and to confirm your spot.

3 Practice mindfulness through food

Meet mindful people in your community over dinner together. The first 15 minutes will be enjoyed in silence.
